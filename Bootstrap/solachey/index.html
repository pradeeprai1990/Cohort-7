About Us

Solachey is a leading brand specializing in modern lighting, illumination solutions, and HVAC supplies. As a global lighting brand with operations based in Kuwait and a growing presence across the Gulf, we proudly serve both trade and contract professionals, as well as everyday consumers. We deliver high-quality, innovative products that meet diverse needs, driven by a deep passion to fulfill our customers’ lighting and space conditioning requirements.
